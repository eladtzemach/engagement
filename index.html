How we met

(According to Jodi)

For those of you who know Elad well, you’ll know he usually starts the story of how we met with him being born in Israel… Well, my version starts a little later, when he moved from Ottawa to Toronto for some more excitement. It didn’t take us long to connect after he arrived (4 days to be exact), thanks to modern dating technology. We had decent texting banter, but it was my excellent joke comparing the rain to Noah’s ark that really sealed the deal and he asked for my number. Our first date lasted hours, including a detailed account of his life story, with me enjoying every minute. Since then he’s retold that story many times over but lucky for me, I’m now part of it :)
